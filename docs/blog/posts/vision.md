# We drive business growth with innovation and open source software

At pibiCo, we believe that sustainable growth stems from the perfect combination of experience, innovation, and technology. Our track record in industrial and business projects is the engine that drives our clients' future.

We empower organizations by helping them transform their processes, improve their efficiency, and remain competitive in a constantly evolving environment.

---

## Business-focused consulting

We support our clients at every stage, providing a practical and results-oriented approach:

- Industrial and strategic plans
- Project audits
- Contract and procurement management
- Business development
- Project monitoring and control

We work closely with each company to understand its real needs and transform them into effective and customized solutions.

---

## Open Source Technology as a Competitive Advantage

Our technological approach is based on **free and open source software**, because we believe in flexibility, scalability, and technological independence.

Our platform is built on a solid and proven stack:
- **Frappe Framework**, as a foundation for agile development
- **ERPNext**, for fully customizable business management
- **Nextcloud**, for secure document management in a private cloud

On this foundation, we develop our own solutions that adapt to the growth of each business.
